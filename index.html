About Me

One Year Ago,


I was paving driveways for a living. Yes, I am a woman, and for 8 years, I loved being a concrete laborer. And I know you're thinking...

Only crazy people love working concrete!

Yep, we sure do!

We're also crazy hard working, crazy dedicated, tough as concrete, solid under pressure, super-duper problem destroyers! That's who you want working for you!


Unfortunately concrete does not work, it only breaks your back. Time to reinvent Angela! Taking the drive and stamina I learned from construction work, I steadfastly dove into web design and development. I removed all impediments, coded daily and I finally achieved my academic self-set goal to become a certified front-end web developer! (Yes, I have certificates.) The Learning and refining these new skills has been wonderfully challenging and fulfilling. The confidence and self-esteem I have gained is joyous and humbling. I found my new love because it's a non-stop learning journey, always improving and reengineering.


I am offering the latest innovations in web design and development, built with a tenacious positive attitude, ridiculous creative vision, fresh, hip coding languages and a custom collaborative process tailored to your web design goals that will leave you 100% in the driver's seat!


Allow me to put these calloused hands to work for you! I take my work seriously, I am meticulous, methodical and I always do my research.
